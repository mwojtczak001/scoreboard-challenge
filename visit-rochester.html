Visit Rochester

Birthplace of Kodak film, nestled alongside the historic Erie Canal, the city is known for its "Garbage Plate" and annual Jazz Festival

Top activities to do in Rochester, NY

Try our famous "garbage plate"!

Created at Nick Tahou's, though there are many great options throughout the city!

Visit the George Eastman Museum!

Founder of Kodak and pioneer of film. Revolutionized photography, film, and motion picture

Visit the Susan B. Anthony House!

Home (now museum) of Susan B. Anthony, national figure in women's rights in the US

Attend the International Jazz Festival

Jazz enthusiasts from around the globe come to Rochester for our annual jazz festival!
